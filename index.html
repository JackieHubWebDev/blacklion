Alicia Marie Phidd is the creator of the "Walking Bank Theory", a leadership doctrine that empowers individuals to monetize their talents and achieve financial independence. She is a nonfiction author, lecturer in leadership and entrepreneurship, and the host of the "Behind The News: The Briefing" podcast. Phidd is the author of several internationally selling books, including "A Lion Minds: The Power of 3,600 Know Your Worth" (2023), "Success Is Subjective: Give Yourself A #Checkmark" (2020), "Business Life Basics: 12 Principles" (2016), "2020DC: Empowerment Through Knowledge" (2020), "God Made This" (2021), "Kingmaker: Personification Of A Masterpiece" (2022), and "A Lion's Tea Party: Motivation For The Entrepreneur" (2023). Phidd holds degrees from SUNY at Stony Brook ( B. Sc. in Chemistry, Master's in Environmental Studies, Graduate Certificate in Solid Waste) and St. Thomas University College of Law (J.D.). When she's not writing, Phidd is actively involved in creating spoken word and its accompanying music. Her motto, "Chance favors the prepared mind!" - Louis Pasteur, inspires her work and the lives she touches. Phidd lives in her creativity, using her diverse background and passion for helping others to make a lasting impact through her teachings, writings, and entrepreneurial pursuits.
